PROBLEMS:
1. Cars are densly packed in the sample video, there's overlap between cars, because of which some cars are recognized
   while others aren't. So some numberplates are not recognized. --- YOLO specific problem

2. In sample video cars are travelling towards the camera, so in one frame a car is far away, YOLO detects the numberplate
   but since it's far, the OCR accuracy isn't great. The same car then comes closer in the later frames giving a much clearer
   view of the numberplate. This will give good OCR accuracy. So the licesence number keeps flickering as the video plays.

Solution - Track the number plate for say 20-30 frames, note all OCR readings. Use majority voting to get most accurate reading.

3. Number plate brightness is low. 

Solution - Basic preprocessing to increase brightness before passing to OCR.

OPTIMIZATIONS:
1. If say at an alphabet location we get a numerical value, then we can make educated guess.
   8 should be B
   1 should be I
   0 should be O
   2 should be Z
   5 should be S
   6 should be G
   and vice versa.
